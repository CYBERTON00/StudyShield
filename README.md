# StudyShield 📚🛡️

StudyShield is a smart productivity and digital wellness app designed especially for students.
The app helps students stay focused, reduce mobile addiction, improve study habits, and maintain a healthy balance between learning and screen time.

---

## 🚀 Problem We Are Solving

Today many students struggle with:

* Mobile addiction
* Social media distractions
* Low concentration
* Poor sleep habits
* Excessive screen time
* Lack of study discipline

StudyShield transforms the smartphone from a distraction into a study companion.

---

# ✨ Features

## 🎯 Smart Focus Mode

Blocks distracting apps like:

* Instagram
* YouTube Shorts
* Games
* Reels apps

during study sessions.

---

## ⏳ AI Study Timer

Pomodoro-based intelligent timer:

* Study sessions
* Short breaks
* Long breaks
* Daily focus goals

---

## 🏆 Reward & Gamification System

Students earn:

* Coins
* Badges
* Streaks
* XP points

for completing focus sessions.

---

## 🌱 Virtual Plant Growth

Your study focus grows a virtual plant.

More focus = healthier plant
More distraction = weak plant

This makes studying fun and motivating.

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Dashboard

Parents can:

* View study progress
* Check focus hours
* Monitor healthy usage

without invading student privacy.

---

## 👀 Eye Care Reminder

Implements the **20-20-20 rule**:
Every 20 minutes,
look 20 feet away
for 20 seconds.

---

## 🌙 Night Protection Mode

Helps students maintain better sleep by:

* Reducing blue light
* Sending bedtime reminders
* Limiting late-night usage

---

## 🤖 AI Distraction Detection

Detects frequent app switching and sends motivational reminders like:

> “Stay focused! Only 15 minutes left 💪”

---

## 👥 Group Study Rooms

Students can:

* Study with friends virtually
* Compete on focus streaks
* Stay accountable together

---

## 📊 Weekly Analytics

Provides reports for:

* Focus hours
* Screen time
* Sleep schedule
* Productivity trends
